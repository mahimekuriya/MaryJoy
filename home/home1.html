who we are

Mary Joy Development Association (MJDA) is a nongovernmental, non religious, non political and nonprofit making organization established under the Civil Societies and Charity Agency of the Democratic Republic of Ethiopia with registration number 060. The idea of MJDA emerged with the inception of the present Executive Director Sister Zebider Zewdie with a remarkable support of other fellows.

PROGRAM AREAS

In Ethiopia there are 718,550 people living with HIV/AIDS.
Mary Joy is doing its part to deal with this
fact by launching programs that focusing on

  • Providing care and support for the people affected
  • Prevention methods
  • Preparation for families in transition
  • Prolonging parent child relations
  • Preserving childrens future

Mary Joy uplifts communities by working on

  • Economic Empowerment
  • Primary health services
  • Primary education

Community and Organizational Capacity Building

  • Institutional capacity building
  • Capacity Building for CBOs and Target Communities

Private community, public partnership

Mary Joy works diligently to ensure institutional
sustainability by enhancing the culture of giving and
volunteering both locally and internationally

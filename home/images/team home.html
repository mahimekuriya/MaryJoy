who we are?

Mary Joy Development Association (MJDA) is a nongovernmental, non religious, non political and nonprofit making organization established under the Civil Societies and Charity Agency of the Democratic Republic of Ethiopia with registration number 060. The idea of MJDA emerged with the inception of the present Executive Director Sister Zebider Zewdie with a remarkable support of other fellows.

Information Education and Communication / Behavior Change Communication [IEC/BCC],
Voluntary Counseling and testing [VCT], Prevention of Mother to Child Transmission [PMTCT],
Condom Promotion and distribution, sexually transmitted infection/STI/ management, organizing and strengthening Anti- AIDS Clubs.

Nutritional and Psycho-social support, personal development and life skill training, monthly cash transfer, provision of casual cloth.

special focus on infected and affected members of the community both through service delivery and advocacy work.

. Above and beyond Local resource mobilization, through family sponsorship is the major focus of the organization
to ensure sustainability and ownership of the program ensure
institutional sustainability and program continuity through enhancing the culture of giving, and upholding local and international resource mobilization.

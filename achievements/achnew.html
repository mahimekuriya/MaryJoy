Volunteer Community Project

For 35 year old Sintayehu Endale, her full-time occupation is improving the well being of people living with HIV/AIDS, destitute children and senior citizens living in her neighborhood.Sintayehu, married with five children, was born in the city of Dire Dawa in 1968. Different impacts and results have been obtained as a result of various integrated development interventions that benefited children, youth, women, older person, People living with the HIV virus and the larger community.In recognition of the vital role played by Mary Joy in the arena of poverty reduction, HIV&AIDS prevention and control, Community capacity building and the various knowledge transfer initiatives, various individuals, non-governmental organizations, government offices and community based organizations have provided and reflected their gratitude and appreciation as an encouragement to its future action.
